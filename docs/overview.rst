
OntoWeaver is a tool that **automatize** the creation of **knowledge graphs**
from **existing data**.

It is made for people who want to **easily define their own graph structure**.
Why having to use a knowledge graph that does not fit the question you are asking
when you can easily make one that you perfectly understands? OntoWeaver allows
you to do that with just a **simple description** of the graph you want.
